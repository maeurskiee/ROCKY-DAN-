Grade 9 - Fairness
AP MONTH 2025

During AP Month, I learned how important it is to be aware of what’s happening in our country and to understand the role we play as citizens. The quiz bee about population development, the seminar about the West Philippine Sea, and the editorial cartoon contest taught me about our rights as a nation, how unity and awareness can protect our sovereignty, and how art can be used to express opinions and raise awareness about issues that violate the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs). I can apply what I learned by staying informed, using my voice responsibly, and promoting peace and fairness in small ways—in school or in our community. I actively participated through quiz bees, seminar, and cartooning, which I enjoyed a lot! If I were to teach this to a classmate, I’d say that AP Month isn’t just about history nor geography, but about understanding the world we live in and realizing that even students like us can make a difference through awareness, nationalism, and action.
