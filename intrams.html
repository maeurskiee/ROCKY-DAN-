Grade 9 - Fairness
INTRAMURALS 2025

From this year's Intramurals, I learned the importance of teamwork, discipline, and sportsmanship—values that go far beyond any game. Winning isn’t everything; what truly matters is how you support your team, manage setbacks, and stay fair even under pressure. I can apply this lesson in real life by working better with others, handling competition maturely, and understanding that success comes through efforts and hard work. I participated actively in the Mr. and Ms. Intramurals by being the "bull" in our candidates' sportswear attire bullfighting. I, with my dance partner, also performed our intermission number in the dancesport competition. Fortunately, our team Yellow Padolina was crowned the overall champion for this event! If I were to explain this to other people, I’d say that Intramurals isn’t just about playing sports—it’s about learning how to face challenges with integrity and grace, emphasizing that Intramurals help students develop character and friendship. This event is important because it builds unity, strengthens friendships, and teaches lessons that apply to every area of life.
